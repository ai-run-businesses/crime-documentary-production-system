## Integration

- Insert the master as one locked unit after the episode-specific cold open.
- Do not add another logo, profile icon, subtitle bar, music bed, speed change, or
  CTA on top of it.
- Use an intentional audio handoff from the cold open. Let the master ending sting
  complete, then begin Act 1 with a J-cut only if it does not cover the sting.
- If the full episode timeline changes, move the whole master; do not trim its head
  or tail.

## Change control

Any replacement requires explicit producer approval, a new versioned working
master, three equal-loudness music auditions when music changes, final-three-frame
QA, a new checksum, and an updated manifest. Do not overwrite the approved file
until the replacement passes all gates.
